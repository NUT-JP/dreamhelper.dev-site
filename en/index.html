Features

Control Start/Stop and settings from the GUI.

Automation runs (Live)

Supports Free Live, Multi Live, and Challenge Live. Helps you run sessions with configurable song pools.

  • Filter non-full-combo songs to collect stars (Free Live)
  • Limit selection to favorites
  • Auto-recover Live Boost (stars / drinks)

Manual Play

Pick a song and difficulty explicitly. Intended for cases like Stage Challenge.

  • Search and select a song
  • Set difficulty and start

Utilities

Automation tasks (e.g., song purchasing, character training tasks)

Known limitations

Timing can drift due to emulator lag, micro-freezes, or noise, making auto-play unstable. Success rate may drop on lower-spec environments.

In Multi Live, duplicate song titles (e.g., Orange, Silhouette President) may cause selection failures in about 50% of cases. Also, Multi Live difficulty is currently fixed to EXPERT (may expand if there's demand).

Requirements

System

FAQ

FAQ

It fails more often on my PC +
Emulator lag and micro-freezes can break timing. Check your resolution and in-game settings, and tune latency/offset settings (e.g., Photogate) if available.
Multi Live fails when there are duplicate song titles +
Due to design limitations, songs with duplicate titles may fail to be selected correctly, with about 50% failure rate. (e.g., Orange, Silhouette President)
Is it safe? Will I get banned? +
Automation may violate the game terms of service. No guarantees can be provided. Use at your own risk.

That said, since the release in October 2025, no bans have been reported.
